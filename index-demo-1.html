Token FAQ

Frequently Questions

Here is a list of questions that have been asked often by people seeking to engage with out community

What are the objectives of this Token?

Crosscoin is an African base utility token that was created by a group of talented men around the globe with the aim of eliminating poverty globally and to cross people from the point of merely dreaming to the point of realising their dreams. The goal of the company is to leverage Blockchain technology in bringing solutions to everyday people. All of the company's products cater to market gaps across various Industries.

How can I buy Crosscoin?

Crosscoin can be bought on the website, peer2peer and on the various markets trading it.

Which Wallet can be used to store Crosscoin?

Crosscoin can be stored inside the Crosscoin wallet through the website or the mobile app.

how may I take part in and purchase this Token?

There are 2 main rounds of sale; the pre-sale round and when the coin gets listed on exchanges. Users can participate in any of those rounds except the presale round which will be limited and constrained to a specific time.

-What's the difference between Bitcoin and Crosscoin?

Bitcoin is the first and most popular cryptocurrency. It was designed to be a medium of exchange and has also evolved to become a store of value cryptocurrency wise. Crosscoin is an altcoin and a Utility token. Utility tokens are created to give people access to products or services. Our products are bringing Blockchain powered solutions to different industries and Crosscoin would be the transactional token for our network of products.

If I buy Crosscoin now, how much profit would I make after two months/ One year?

This is a popular question that we have been getting from community members. It is important to note that the value of Crosscoin cannot be pegged to a particular amount based on timelines.

How do I earn money using cross coin

The value of a utility token greatly depends on the network of products that is backing it. This is why we are very Intentional about building products and services that have market demand and would solve the problems people are facing in their respective Industries.

The first way to earn from Crosscoin is to buy and Hold it for the long term. While holding it for Long Term it is best you stake it ( like fixed deposit) so that you can earn Interest on the Crosscoin you are holding. You can stake for a minimum of 6 months and a maximum of 56 months.

You would be earning an APR(Annual Percentage Rate of 1% monthly (i.e 12% in a Year) Also if you are someone who knows how to trade Cryptocurrency you can earn on exchanges where Crosscoin is listed by actively trading Crosscoin.
